Buldak Noodles

Description:

Buldak noodles or Fire noodles as they are also known are noodles from South Korea that are known for their spicy taste and ease of making. In order to make Buldak noodles, you need to buy the noodle packet online. Open the packet and add the noodles to boiling water, and cook them for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, drain the excess water, and add the buldak sauce provided in the packet. Add toppings as required. To enhance the recipe, you can also add barbeque chicken or cheese to cut down on the heat a bit.

Ingredients

  • Buldak Noodles Packet
  • Water to boil the noodles
  • Optional barbeque chicken
  • Optional cheese
